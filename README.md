# Amazon-Sales-Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview  
This project presents an interactive **Amazon Sales Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze sales performance, customer preferences, and operational efficiency.  

The dashboard provides insights into **product categories, order fulfillment, delivery performance, customer size preferences, and regional demand**, enabling data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Analyze overall sales performance  
- Identify top-performing product categories  
- Understand customer purchase patterns (size, region)  
- Evaluate order fulfillment and delivery efficiency  
- Detect data gaps and operational inefficiencies  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies  
- **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation  
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures and KPIs  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features  
- KPI card displaying **Total Sales Amount (11.58M)**  
- Category-wise sales distribution  
- Courier status breakdown (Shipped, On the Way, Cancelled, Unshipped)  
- Fulfillment analysis (Amazon vs Merchant)  
- Delivery partner insights  
- Size-wise demand analysis  
- State-wise sales distribution  
- Interactive date filter (slicer)  

---

## 📊 Key Insights  

- **High Category Dependence:**  
  Sales are heavily concentrated in *T-shirts and Shirts*, indicating limited product diversification and potential revenue risk.  

- **Strong Shipping Performance:**  
  Approximately **85% of orders are shipped**, reflecting efficient order processing and logistics operations.  

- **Fulfillment Strategy:**  
  Around **65% of orders are fulfilled by Amazon**, showing strong reliance on platform-managed logistics.  

- **Data Quality Concern:**  
  A large portion of delivery partner data is labeled as *“Unknown”*, indicating potential data tracking or integration issues.  

- **Customer Size Preference:**  
  Demand is highest for **M, XL, and XXL sizes**, suggesting the need for inventory optimization toward mid-to-larger sizes.  

- **Regional Sales Concentration:**  
  States like **Maharashtra and Karnataka** contribute the highest number of orders, indicating strong performance in metro regions.  

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations  
- Diversify product offerings beyond top-performing categories  
- Optimize inventory based on high-demand sizes (M–XL range)  
- Improve data tracking for delivery partners  
- Expand marketing efforts in underperforming regions  
- Reduce dependency on a few categories to minimize risk
